Eligibility Checker

Basic requirements to donate blood in the Philippines:

Age between 18 and 65 years old
Weight at least 50 kg (110 lbs)
Hemoglobin level ≥ 12.5 g/dL
No illness or fever in the last 2 weeks
No tattoos or piercings in the last 12 months
No blood donation in the last 3 months
